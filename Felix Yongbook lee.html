Felix Yoogbok Lee

Felix Yongbok Leeborn 15 September 2000, known as mononymously as Felix, is an Australian rapperand singer based in the South Korea.He is a memeber of the South Korean boy band Stray Kids formed by JYP Entertainment in 2017

Early Life

Felix Yongbok Lee was born on 15 September 2000 in Seven Hills,Syden, Australia to korean immigrants parents. He is the middle child between his younger and older sister.Before moving to Seoul,South Korea to become a singer,Felix practiced taekwando for twelve years as a child and has won a total of 63 medals.He studied at St Patrick's Marist college in Sydney and began his training at JYP Entertainment at age 16

Career

Felix begans as a trainee at JYP Entertainment in February 2017.He was personally chosen by Stray Kids' leader,Bang Chan making him the sixth memeber of the band to be selected.Felix continued to train with Stray Kids until the brodcast of the survival show, also titled Stray Kids.During this show,he faced elimination in the eighth epsode,decided by Park Jin-young
